Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

At HTMLtoImages, we prioritize your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your data.

1. Zero Data Collection

We are proud to operate as a client-side only tool. When you use HTMLtoImages to convert your code into images, no code or images are sent to our servers. All processing occurs locally in your web browser.

2. Local Storage

We may use local storage or cookies solely to remember your preferences (like editor theme or last used settings) to improve your experience. No personal identifiers are stored.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use standard analytics tools (like Google Analytics) to understand website traffic trends. These tools collect anonymous data such as page views and browser types, but do not access your generated content.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at htmltoimages@gmail.com.
